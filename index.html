About PAU

Prakash Agarbatti Udyog

Established with a vision to bring premium quality incense sticks to every home, Prakash Agarbatti Udyog (PAU) has been a pioneer in the industry for over two decades.

Our products are crafted with the finest natural ingredients, sourced from sustainable suppliers. We take pride in our traditional manufacturing techniques that have been passed down through generations, combined with modern quality control measures.

At PAU, we believe that fragrances have the power to transform moods, spaces, and experiences. Our diverse range of aromas caters to different preferences and occasions, from meditation and prayer to home freshening and aromatherapy.
